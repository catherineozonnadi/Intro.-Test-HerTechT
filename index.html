HER VOL.3

By Catherine ozonnadi

Published on July 4, 2024

I've always wanted to be the kind of person who radiates positivity and joy, and I've come to realize that gratitude is the key to unlocking that mindset. So, I've made it my mission to cultivate a sense of gratitude in my daily life.

I started by keeping a gratitude journal, where I write down three things I'm thankful for each day. It's amazing how this simple practice has shifted my focus away from stress and anxiety and towards the good things in my life.
I'm also making a conscious effort to express my gratitude to others, whether it's a kind word, a text message, or a small gift. I want to be the kind of person who lifts others up and makes them feel seen and appreciated.

As I continue on this journey, I'm learning that gratitude is a muscle that needs to be exercised regularly. Some days it comes easily, while others it's a struggle. But I'm determined to keep at it, because I know that the more I practice gratitude, the more it will become a part of who I am. My goal is to become a person who embodies gratitude and spreads joy to those around me. It's a journey, not a destination, and I'm excited to see where it takes me.
